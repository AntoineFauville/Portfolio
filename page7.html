The asteroid’s research outpost.

Unity Editor - 2019

I tried to make a realistic and believable experience on Unity using modular design to create the space station.
This is an outpost build on an asteroid. The focus of the experience is future realism.

Building a location that made sense, a believable research outpost. A station assembled by modules as if humans would build this for a small expedition. For this I created few modular set pieces to create the station and assemble them. Trying to stick as much as I could to real room utilities.

This is a first mission out of 3 missions. The location takes place in a research outpost based in an asteroid field . Two researchers, Mary and Alex Kurhy, married, have been sent by Dave Klaston, a metal dealer, to study an asteroid potential component. Rare minerals could be found on it, the suspens builds up until you find them dead, you run to your space ship to go back to Dave Klaston to tell him what happened.
