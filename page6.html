Far Cry 5 Mods

Under Mary's Church

Far Cry Editor - 2019

24h realisation with document. Open World Far Cry 5 alike Outpost.

Here I created something that could fit in the Far Cry 5 universe. Open World Map, an outpost to liberate by killing all enemies in it, Having a lot of vantage points and multiple access to enter or navigate to the camp, lot of zip lines to utilise the player abilities. It supports Stealth, Assault and long range type of players. The church act as a memorable monument and a landmark.

I was also writing down some documentation during the process.

LumberJack Camp

Far Cry Editor - 2018

The LumberJack Camp is a map I made on the far cry 5 arcade editor. I wanted a place to create and publish content to get better at prototyping environment and maps. Far cry 5 editor was providing me an easy-to-use content in a fast pace interface where I could publish my maps directly and get after 1 week an average feedback on how people liked the map.

I started with a pitch of an environment but most of all I wanted to create something that could be believable to be in the Far Cry 5. Open World Map, a camp to liberate by killing all enemies in it.
