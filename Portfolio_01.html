Bombslinger (Switch / Xbox / PC)

June 2017 - April 2017 - July and Augustus 2016

  • Made with Unity by Mode4
  • Bombslinger is a Bomberman-inspired Wild West action game with bombs! A solo hardcore reinvention of the iconic explosive action!
    A Rogue-like, Dungeon Crawler with randomly generated monsters and level, boss.

    FallBot (PC / Arcade)

    October 2016

  • Made with Unity for Ludus Creative Game Jam
  • FallBot You are the two remaining robot of the humanity, G.O.R.G (Galactic Observation and Repair Guardian) and N.I.C.O (Networked Immediate Calculation Organism) trying to explore space with the last earthling plant left. But during your travel through the galaxy, your spaceshift will hit a meteor and N.I.C.O. will be trapped in it. You play both robots, one is trapped in the center of the meteor with the last plant, the second is trying to save him.
  • Game Design / Pixel Artist and Animator "Pixel Museum – Opening (Group), Pixel Museum – Schiltigheim, Schiltigheim – Strasbourg – France
    We got selected to be one of the game that would be showned at the opening of the pixel museum of strasbourg with the game “FallBot”

    Industrial Entreprise

    2015

  • Made with Unity for ESA ST-Luc
  • Industrial Entreprise This was a small project made for a school demand. Second year project at Saint-Luc ESA in Saint-Gilles, Brussels. Digital Arts Studies.
    Assigment :
    - Theme : Utopia.
    - Build a flat with an ambiance and some personnal esthetic.
    - At least 3 interactions.
    - made in Unity and in 3D.
    I did add an extra room if you activate everything, this room was an other exercice. Tribute to an art piece. There is no goal.
  • My intention here was to create a space and ask questions about this space What we are going to be living in, in 2030 ? This is a dystopia where everyone have the same flat, everyone working at the same place (It’s a critic of some actual social system).
